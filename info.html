What is D&D

Dungeons and Dragons or commonly known as D&D is one of the most popular TRPGs. Published by Wizards of the Coast, the game has developed over 40 years and has released many different books and other products. Many of these books are guides that aid the players and DMs in playing or creating games called campaigns. Some of these books are also prebuilt campaigns that allow DMs to skip the more tedious part of worldbuilding and creation of many differnt NPCs. Other products include video games, board games, and accessories as well. How D&D became popular.
